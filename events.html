The Proposition

A place, not a venue.

Valinor Ranch was not built to host events. It was built to hold what matters. That the land, the architecture, and the privacy happen to create the conditions for an extraordinary celebration is something you will feel when you arrive.

No packages. No templates. Every gathering here begins with a conversation about what the occasion means to you. The ranch shapes itself around that answer.

What This Place Offers

The setting, the architecture, the privacy.

The Setting

High meadow on Missouri Heights, with uninterrupted sight lines to Mt. Sopris and the Elk Mountain Range. A landscape that does not need decoration.

The Architecture

A modern home with glass scaled to frame the mountains from every room. A structure that complements the land without competing with it.

The Privacy

One group at a time. No shared spaces. No neighbouring guests. The ranch is entirely yours for the duration of your celebration.

The Accommodation

Two bedrooms and two bathrooms in the ranch house. For larger parties, nearby accommodations are available in Carbondale and the Roaring Fork Valley — ten minutes from the property.
