Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our website, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, email address, phone number, preferred dates, guest count, and details about your occasion or stay.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or reservation request
  • Communicate details about your stay or event
  • Improve our services and website

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing unless you have explicitly opted in.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to process your booking (e.g., payment processors or booking platforms) or as required by law.

4. Cookies and Analytics

This site may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. These tools collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and referring URLs. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party services such as booking platforms or social media. These services have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. Colorado residents may have additional rights under the Colorado Privacy Act.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect upon posting to this page.
